Networking

Computer networking is the practice of connecting computers and other devices to share resources and communicate. Networking relies on protocols such as TCP/IP, HTTP, and DNS to define how data is transmitted. The internet is the largest computer network in the world, connecting billions of devices globally.

Web development depends heavily on networking concepts such as HTTP requests, REST APIs, and WebSockets. Databases often run on separate servers and communicate with applications over a network. Operating systems provide built-in networking stacks that applications use to send and receive data.
